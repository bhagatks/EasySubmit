What is EasySubmit.ai?

EasySubmit.ai is a job application platform. Users tailor resumes to job postings, check ATS compatibility, track applications in a dashboard, and capture jobs with a Chrome extension.

What EasySubmit.ai does

  • Tailor resumes to each job description with AI assistance
  • Export ATS-safe PDF and Word resumes
  • Score keyword gaps, bullet quality, and parse integrity
  • Track job applications from saved to applied
  • Capture jobs and preview tailored resumes with a Chrome extension

How EasySubmit.ai uses Google sign-in

When you sign in with Google, EasySubmit.ai requests your email address, name, and profile photo to create your account and authenticate you. EasySubmit.ai does not read Gmail, Google Drive, Calendar, Contacts, or other Google user data.
